About the movie Ratatouille

Ratatouille is a movie about a Rat named Remy who lives with his family in a womans attic just outside of Paris. He has a heightened sense of smell and a fasination for cooking. Remy dreams to become a chef in Gusteau's Resturaunt. He later gets seperated from the rest of his family when the woman who owns the house discovers them in the attic and they escape. Remy after being lost finds himself on top of the roof to Gusteau's resturaunt.
Remy enters into the resturaunt to see Alfredo Linguini adding ingrediants to the soup and Remy runs over to fix it. Linguini catches Remy in the act but does not tell anyone because Remy made the soup really good. Remy and Linguini start to work together so Remy can cook and linguini can have a job.

Director: Brad Bird & Jan Pinkava

Release date: June 29th 2007

Produced by: Pixar Studios
