Review of the movie Ratatouille

Zachary Wyeth

February 7th 2023


Zachary Wyeth 2/8/2023

★★★★★

Ratatouille is an outstanding piece of work. The story follows a clear idea and stays with that idea throughout the whole movie. The animation team for this production did a spectacular job being one of the earlier pixar films. The story following Remys point of view adds lots of more third person views on a lot of things throughout the movie. The entire movie may seen like it follows one idea but it has a bigger deeper meaning.
Ratatouille shows how we can achieve anything they want in life if they work together. It is a beautiful show of teamwork but still keeps it interesting with the work space drama and the outside problems. Ratatouille is an absolutley amazing movie and it was definetly proven with a rating of 96% on rotten tomato.
